# 📋 Cosmic Entry

- **ID:** MV-Z04
- **Name:**  The Shadow
- **Classification:** [Apparition](pages/guides/dimensionguide.md)
- **Difficulty Level:** [None](pages/guides/dimensionguide.md)
- **Risk Level:** [Volatile](pages/guides/dimensionguide.md)

# 📑 Description

The shadow is an extremely rare interdimensional event that can occur as a side effect of using the mindverse too much, too long, and too often.
They appear as silhouette of people made of darkness, usually only rough outlines, they do not have facial features or eyes.
They can fully interact with you, they can touch you, you can touch them, and they are always hostile or trying to hurt you physically in some way.
They cannot actually harm or kill you, but whatever they do to you feels just like if it were actually happening.
They are accompanied by horrible feelings of impending doom, like something bad is going to happen to you and you are about to die.
These descriptions are oddly similar to what one might experience under extreme delirium, and what's even more odd is if you enter the mindverse during this event they can no longer harm you.
It is recommended to try to escape to either the dreamscape, or the mindverse, as soon as possible if you encounter this event.

# 📝 Notes

> I don't understand how they are actually able to physically touch you, they can only harm you mentally, not kill or cause actual damage.
> As if they are just simulating touch or sensations from your brain itself and not your actual body.
