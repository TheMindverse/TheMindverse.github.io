# 📑 Description

The exact same world, people, places, and creatures, as the lord of the rings, seemingly a couple decades after the destruction of the one ring.
The most breathtaking like dimension I've explored.
Strangely, this world has an [ethereal](pages/guides/dimensionguide.md) classification when one would assume it would be [spectral](pages/guides/dimensionguide.md) due to the nature of the fantasy and magic elements, but there is a very strong cosmic and brain connection which could suggest millions of years from now in a distant galaxy a lord of the rings like world could actually exist.
