- **Risk Level:** [Challenging](pages/guides/dimensionguide.md)

# 📑 Description

The voices are a rare interdimensional event that may occur as a result of using the mindverse, specifically rerouting the signals that feed your brain.
They appear like they are muffled slightly like they are coming from inside the walls of your house, most of the time they are a garbled mess and you can't really make out what they are saying.
Other times they may be very threatening, or insulting you with bursts of anger.
They are associated with feelings of impending doom, similar to [the shadow](pages/dimensions/mv-z04.md), this event is strangely comparable to delirium, but without the mental states that are common from delirium such as confusion or memory loss.
Despite the hostile nature of the voices they cannot actually do you any mental or physical harm.
They can cause some distress and major annoyance depending on what they are saying.

# 📝 Notes

> Never listen to them or try to understand what they are saying.
