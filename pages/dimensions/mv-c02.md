# 📑 Description

An exact replica of the universe and planets of astroneer from [earth prime](pages/dimensions/mv-a00.md), infinitely generating caves and terrain with unique biomes to explore.
Lots of minerals and plant life, most of which are hostile as an act of self defense.

# 📝 Notes

> Just like ⁠[hoxxes iv](pages/dimensions/mv-c01.md)﻿ it is pretty hard to navigate to here, I've also visited here while on dissociatives which causes you fly around the planets and caves at a constant rate, you can't really stop at a specific spot as you're always flying.
> Dissociatives aren't required to visit here unlike ⁠⁠[hoxxes iv](pages/dimensions/mv-c01.md), although it is more difficult.
> I've done it once or twice before while meditating and listening to the astroneer soundtrack at the same time.
