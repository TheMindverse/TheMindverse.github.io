# 📑 Description

All of our consciousnesses and life forms work together as a massive hivemind, unknowingly powering the universe by solving internal algorithms necessary for the universe to exist and grow, this is done in physical form on a cellular level such as brain neurons working together, and on a conscious level with our consciousness acting as a neuron in a way to the universe.
It is not known of the machinescapes created the universe as a way to power it, or if its a byproduct of consciousness forming for the first time as life came into existence.
One theory is as life came to be, the cosmic flow that connects us all could have started working as a hivemind and created the machinescapes, and now both have grown so big they work together in a perfect balance.

# 📝 Notes

> It almost gives the opposite feeling of ⁠[the backrooms](pages/dimensions/mv-e00.md)﻿, instead of being hostile it feels like it welcomes you to view, like it knows you and it's fine with you visiting and viewing.
> It knows you are no threat as you cannot conceive the state of its existence or reality.
> The only time you can truly enter the machinescapes is through psychedelics, as they let you see the internal algorithms that make up your consciousnesses through complex patterns.
