# 📑 Description

A world where the only land animal are cats, as a result they have gotten exceptionally good at swimming and hunting for fish.
The cats are very friendly, perhaps due to possibly tens of thousands of years of not having any natural predators they evolved out of being hostile and are all friends with each other.
Maybe humans did exist at once point, along with other animals, but they died out somehow; there's no way to know without digging around trying to find fossils.

# 📝 Notes

> A very cute and fun world to spend time in if you like cats, if you had cats at one point in your life they exist here too if you find the right version of this dimension.
> I found both of mine in here and I often come back to visit them to see how they're doing.
