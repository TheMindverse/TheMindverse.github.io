# 📑 Description

Just like [hoxxes iv](pages/dimensions/mv-c01.md) in nature of it being the same game from [earth prime](pages/dimensions/mv-a00.md), as well as there being a lack of creatures / entities.
Almost all the hostile ones that spawn at night don't exist, and the passive ones are very limited (like pigs, sheep, and cows).
It's an interesting place to walk around in, you finally get a true scale of what its like to be in an infinite world of biomes, structures, and cool terrain.

# 📝 Notes

> My favorite thing to do in this dimension actually isn't building, its explore all the biomes and caves; the atmosphere and vibe they give off is very cozy.
> Same with villages, they are a very chill place to hang out in, villagers do exist in this world despite the lack of almost all other entities.
> Like ⁠[hoxxes iv](pages/dimensions/mv-c01.md)﻿, the only times I've managed to travel here is while on dissociatives.
