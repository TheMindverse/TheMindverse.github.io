# 📑 Description

Many different versions and variations of this world exist, but this is the closest one I could find in the same time and technological period as [earth prime](pages/dimensions/mv-a00.md).
In this world, everyone is guaranteed to have a soulmate and and instant match, no one is single, and a lot of the world and public buildings are focused around love and hearts.
Nothing too special about it other than that.

# 📝 Notes

> I'm not sure how the soulmate thing is guaranteed or how that even works, maybe everyone is just similar enough to each other where common interests, hobbies, and careers, are all relatable instead of everyone being unique.
> Their brain chemistry just might be different or more "predictable" from a genetic standpoint when it comes to personality traits and emotions.
