# 📑 Description

The mirror is another rare interdimensional event that can occur as a result of spending too much time in [the flow](pages/dimensions/mv-z01.md) without traveling anywhere.
In combination with deadwalking, when looking into a mirror and staring at your own face without moving it slowly starts to twist and become contorted.
Your face melts and forms facial expressions that seem impossible, like you're looking at another person in control.
If you start replicating the faces in the mirror, which can happen accidently if you don't pay close enough attention, the faces will take full control.
You will no longer be only seeing them in the mirror, you will start doing it outside of looking into the mirror, and you will act psychotic.
It is almost borderline insanity, which can be incredibly hard to stop as you want to just lose control and let whatever crazed or demonic faces take control of your mood and actions.
You can snap out of it easily enough, but the mood it puts you in can make it hard to want to stop because of how natural it feels.
