# 📑 Description

Despite the fact that the technology is at least 100 years above [earth prime](pages/dimensions/mv-a00.md), the people here saw ancient geek architecture as peak societal design.
After the entire planet banned non-renewable energy it opened the pathway to continuously improving the quality of life for the general population, at one point redesigning every building and instilling greek architecture as a building code requirement for federal and state owned businesses.
Most residential and private properties soon followed the same trend, typical residential homes you might see in [earth prime](pages/dimensions/mv-a00.md) do still exist but only in very rural areas, almost all cities have been greekified.

# 📝 Notes

> A very unique combination of architecture and futuristic solar punk technology, amazing food and public services, everyone is friendly, makes for a great vacation spot.
