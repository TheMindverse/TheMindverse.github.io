# 📑 Description

Almost the exact same as the walking dead show from [earth prime](pages/dimensions/mv-a00.md), only without the characters from the show and places.
The world itself, the virus, time period, are all follow the same logic and rules as the show.
Perhaps there could be a dimension where rick actually exists and follows the same story, but the changes of locating a parallel world where its the exact same sounds impossible.

# 📝 Notes

> One of my favorite dimensions to hang out in, no responsibilities, can loot anything and go anywhere, scavenge for food and build shelter, its everything you could want in a zombie apocalypse world.
> Building up defenses on a home and wandering into down for weapons and supplies are the most fun, but between that there's not much to do for entertainment.
