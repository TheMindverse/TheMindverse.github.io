# 📑 Description

An exact replica of the world from the game genshin impact from [earth prime](pages/dimensions/mv-a00.md), very beautiful landscapes and cities, even all the same characters and people.
There are also challenges and domains to do with rewards, the first time I discovered them I had no clue what they were, the combat is much harder and mentally demanding than you would might expect.
There is a lot of cool places and fun to be had here, but there are some real difficult dangers.
