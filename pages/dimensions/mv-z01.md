# 📑 Description

The flow is what connects everything in the multiverse, and the universes themselves in each dimension, together.
It's what allows for us to travel with the mindverse to other dimensions, its existence is comparable to string theory but on a cosmic scale and not just a universal or particle one.
The flow most likely exists in a forth dimensional space, the multiverse as a whole happening infinitely all at the same time could be what reality actually is, all possible outcomes as a result of quantum superposition outside of our reality.
As three dimensional beings we can only conceive it from a universal perspective.
What the flow is made out of and how it works is not possible to conceptualize for beings constructed in the multiverse, this would require an understanding of a space and time beyond all known laws of science and rules.

# 📝 Notes

> It feels almost like a very ancient and mystical power, I'm not sure if we're even supposed to use it to navigate or travel to other dimensions but it doesn't seem to mind.
