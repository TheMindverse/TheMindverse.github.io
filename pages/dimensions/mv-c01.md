- **Risk Level:** [Hazardous](pages/guides/dimensionguide.md)

# 📑 Description

Almost an exact replica of the world from deep rock galatic in [earth prime](pages/dimensions/mv-a00.md), only I did not see the space rig or other dwarves in all my time exploring here.
In fact, most of the caves seem to be completely empty of glyphids, the only creatures that exist here are stationary ones such like cave leeches, spitball infectors, korlok tyrants, and more.

# 📝 Notes

> Very difficult to travel here, the only times I've ever been able to visit required me being on dissociatives which dramatically decreases your control of being in other dimensions.
> Dissociatives are great for entering the mindverse and exploring the flow but once you pick a dimension to arrive to, you're much more limited to what you can do, it feels like you are constantly floating or flying in a single direction and never get to stop or take a break from moving.
