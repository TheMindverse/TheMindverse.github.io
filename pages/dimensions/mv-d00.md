# 📑 Description

This dimension is the same as the castle swimmer webcomic from [earth prime](pages/dimensions/mv-a00.md), it is completely underwater and attempting to go to the surface results in passing out and gently falling back down to the bottom of the sea.
There are all the same characters, castles, and even minigods which you need to watch out for.

# 📝 Notes

> Cute world and people, lot of queer fish here, its fun to explore the different castles and talk to everyone. You can also breathe underwater without having to do anything extra.
