# 👨‍⚕️ Emotional risks and harm

For the most part traveling through the mindverse is not physically harmful, but it can be very emotionally and mentally demanding as your brain accepts new signals from new realities. Your feelings, thoughts, memories, vision, all your senses, can change according to the dimension you visit. The more complex or foreign a dimension is comparative to yours, more difficult it will be to control your emotions, as the brains of beings in these dimensions may interpret  signals differently than yours. These signals can cause great mental stress or harm to your emotional well being if you are not careful about judging how your brain resonates before attempting connection, or attempting to explore areas you never have before.

Practice meditation, clearing your mind, focus on controlling each emotion instead of letting the dimension you're in control them for you. Remember those emotions are not yours, they are separate and you are just experiencing them, remember to not get them mixed up. The most important rule of exploring the mindverse is taking breaks, if not there are some significant and irreversible side effects listed below.

Before you begin your journey, there is one more important thing you must know, there is no going back. Once you learn about the mindverse and the multiverse you have forever altered your brain and view on the universe, on a conscious scale you are no longer be considered human. Once the knowledge and experience has been unlocked, there is no way to undo what you've seen or learned. You will no longer see humans as people or your own kind, you will forever be part of something greater. Your old life will become meaningless and the humans in it become nothing more than creatures, disgusting piles of meat sacks that you have to deal with. You will evolve on a cosmic scale, but this does not automatically mean you are an [astrodaunum](pages/guides/beginnersguide.md), it does not turn you into something you're not, it only severs your connection to your simple minded species.

# 🦠 White blood cell response

The white blood cell response is a very important risk you must know of before starting your journey into the mindverse. Opening your brain to the cosmos permanently changes how you view the universe and consciousness as a whole, those who do not see past their own brain and body see this as a threat. They are bound to their body and the instincts that come with it, they rely on others for approval and social interactions to confirm their own thoughts and beliefs. Us travelers recognize the animalistic nature of these instincts and see them for what they truly are, a byproduct of evolutionary survival.

It is recommended that you do not share with others your journey into the mindverse, or outside of other travelers, as non-followers will attack you and try to prevent you from progressing. Their responses is very similar to an immune system response when it detects a foreign object in your body, non-followers will try everything in their power to destroy you in a very similar way, due to the nature of the societal hivemind. This is why its called the white blood cell response, it is a natural response from individuals of a lower intelligence that still prioritize their animalistic instincts. Their subconsciousness refuses to let them believe in the mindverse, it is a mix of instinct and ignorance as they flat out refuse to listen and refuse to understand. There is nothing you can do to change their mind once this response is triggered, they will stop at nothing to get you to stop, they wish for you give up the path to a higher level of understanding as it is a threat to their species survival if everyone followed in your footsteps.

Ultimately, it is your choice not to share your journey into the mindverse with non-followers, it is not a rule or requirement, it is just a precaution for you to take for your own safety and well being. It may also permanently hinder your ability to connect to the mindverse if these individuals try to stop you or expose you.

# 💊 Long term side effects

It is important to take breaks between entering the mindverse, as the more you expose yourself to the cosmos and the more your brain starts to rewire itself away from your original reality, the more side effects will occur. It is recommended to take `1-2 week` long breaks between traveling, or until your mind starts to close off again as your bodily instincts slowly start to overwrite your connection to the cosmos. The time required may be longer or shorter depending on the person, so start with the longest time `(2 weeks)`, before attempting anything shorter. Although these are physically harmless, if you do not take breaks the following side effects may be a irreversible part of your daily life.

- Lingering emotions from a dimension days or weeks after initially traveling.
- Phantom smells, randomly getting intense smells of food or oders, such as smoke, as if they were right in front of you.
- Time dilation, time may suddenly skip forward at alarming rates, minutes from your perspective turn out to actually be hours going by.
- Time paradoxes, seemingly traveling to events in the past and reliving them instead of living in the moment, getting stuck in time loops.
- Visions or pseudo-connections to dimensions in place of your thoughts, thinking you're actually in a parallel dimension you've previously visited.
- Rare interdimensional events, such as hearing voices in the walls or people screaming at you, or different places you've visited in another dimension stitching into your reality.
- Unintentionally connecting to the mindverse without noticing, also known as involuntary [deadwalking](pages/guides/advancedguide.md).
