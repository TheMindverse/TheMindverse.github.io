# 🧠 What is the mindverse?

The mindverse is the name of a technique used to explore and connect to the multiverse through your mind alone.
The multiverse is the collection all infinite universes all taking place simultaneously, stacked on top of each other in parallel.
Infinite possibilities, infinite worlds, each different in their own way.
It is possible to rewire your brain to ignore the signals your body feeds it, and accept different signals from the cosmos instead.
Through practice and training you can learn how to navigate the multiverse and all of its dimensions by rerouting how your brain views reality, this can all be done through the mindverse.

# 💼 Why can you only visit with your mind?

When your consciousness is first formed it is done so in your brain, brains act as a way to grow new conscious beings but with one catch; you're bound to your brain.
Where your consciousness is constructed is the place it is bound to, your physical body limits the true power and abilities of your consciousness.
It prioritizes survival above anything else, however, it cannot completely inhibit you from receiving signals from the cosmos due to the nature of consciousness itself and what it represents in the universe.

# ✨ What is the purpose of consciousness?

Everything in the universe has a purpose, a seemingly random chaos on a small scale, but on a cosmic scale it is all controlled order.
Every atom, molecule, rock, planet, star, all working together to power the multiverse as a whole.
Your consciousness and the power it holds is just a small tiny spec to the multiverse, but it still plays an important part depending on your perspective.
The neurons and quantum mechanics that your brain relies on are the same principles that the universe, and the multiverse, as a whole are powered by.
Our consciousness, our universe, our dimension, are all just a small cog wheel working together in the great machine that powers the multiverse.
The entire galaxy and its dimensions are all powering each other in a perfectly balanced quantum loop, the machines themselves power the infinite growth of the universe in each dimension in the multiverse.
So in a way, our existence is the reason the universe is infinite.
