# 🪔 Meditation practices

Meditation can help you learn to control your mindset and emotions better, which is necessary if you wish to connect to the mindverse.
It is important to be alone when doing meditation practices as you must be fully closed off from the societal hivemind.
Practice controlling your emotions, clearing your head of all thoughts, practice feeling your brains inner hum, try to visualize the algorithmic patterns that power your consciousness.
See beyond what your physical body feeds your brain.

# 💤️ Exploring the dreamscape

Entering the dreamscape is the first step to take to learn how to open your mind, as the mental control required to do so is a good introduction into learning how to form new connections.
Using the skills you learned when practicing meditation and controlling mental states, try to visualize pathways to other dimensions while dreaming, focus on feeling the hum of your brain as it enters REM sleep.
The hum and how it feels is the mental state you need to be in to phase into the dreamscape, you need to take control of your brains  algorithmic patterns to learn how your brain receives signals.

It is easier to do this asleep as this is when your imagination is at its peak, as the technique of rerouting your physical body into your emotions, and controlling your emotions in return, is a similar process to rerouting your brain to accept the signals from the cosmos instead of your body.
Learn to see by just feeling the new signals your brain is receiving, not by your eyes. Check out [the dreamscape](pages/dimensions/mv-z03.md) for more info on its existence.
