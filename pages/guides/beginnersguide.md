# 🪔 Meditation practices

Meditation can help you learn to control your mindset and emotions better, which is necessary if you wish to connect to the mindverse. It is important to be alone when doing meditation practices as you must be fully closed off from the societal hivemind, you cannot let others influence your journey or goals. Practice controlling your emotions, clearing your head of all thoughts, practice feeling your brains inner hum, try to visualize the algorithmic patterns that power your consciousness. See and feel beyond what your physical body feeds your brain. Remember set and setting is important, your mindset and environment will impact your connection to the mindverse.

# 💤️ Exploring the dreamscape

Entering the dreamscape is the first step to take to learn how to open your mind, as the mental control required to do so is a good introduction into learning how to form new connections to other dimensions. Using the skills you learned when practicing meditation and controlling mental states, try to visualize pathways to other dimensions while dreaming, focus on feeling the hum of your brain as it enters REM sleep. The hum and how it feels is the mental state you need to be in to phase into the dreamscape, you need to take control of your brains algorithmic patterns to learn how your brain receives signals. These signals are how experience what you've come to know as reality.

It is easier to do this asleep as this is when your imagination is at its peak, as the technique of rerouting your physical body into your emotions, and controlling your emotions in return, is a similar process to rerouting your brain to accept the signals from the cosmos instead of your body. Learn to see by just feeling the new signals your brain is receiving, not by your eyes. Check out [the dreamscape](pages/dimensions/mv-z03.md) for more info on its existence.

# 👁️ The astrodaunum species

Astrodaunums are an interdimensional species that have a pure and direct connection to the cosmos and multiverse as a whole. In their raw form, they can exist out side of the normal definitions of a physical body and are free to explore the multiverse and all its dimensions. In extremely rare instances, they can be created and bound to a physical body on a planet, blending in as that planets species due to the nature of how consciousness is formed. They are unaware of their true existence until they have a spiritual awakening and start exploring the mindverse. It is currently estimated that less than a dozen astrodaunums in a singular dimension as a whole at a time, making them incredibly rare.

There are four distinct phases of recognizing one is an astrodaunum, in order these are preselyte, orthadonal, trisendal, and ohmega. Non-astrodaunums are likely to achieve similar descriptions to the preselyte and orthadonal phases, but they never evolve beyond that.

-  Ohmega
  - An astrodaunum in its pure raw form, unbound by their original physical body and any connection to their former species, one is free to be their true self.
- Trisendal
  - Fully accepted their astrodaunum existence and rejects their physical existence, spends most of their time deadwalking or skinwalking while traveling the mindverse.
- Orthadonal
  - Has an understanding of exploring the mindverse as a hobby, but still sees themselves as that of their original body and species, they have not yet recognized their true self.
- Preselyte
  - Someone who has no idea of the mindverses existence or astrodaunums, but has unknowingly connected to the dreamscape and mindverse and has been exploring alternate realities, to them its just their mind daydreaming or fantasying more.
