> I had a nightmare last night I was on a submarine above the deepest part of the ocean, I forget what happened exactly but something went wrong and we lost power and there was a leak. One side filled with water and we started floating downward pointed straight down. We were waiting for some kind of backup power to kick in or something and it just never happened.

> We kept falling down and down and eventually we knew we were going to die at the bottom of the coean

> For some reason I remember it feeling like the air was getting heavier and heavier and preassure was building, I remember slowly dying. It felt like my head was slowly exploding and I was suffocating, my vision was getting dark and it was a super slow painful death as we ran out of oxygen and the pressure kept getting heavier and heavier.

> It was pretty terrifying and the worst part was the very slow and painful death while we were basically upside down with no way out.
