- **Date:** June 17th, 2024
- **Body Weight:** 108 Pounds
- **Dosage:** 300mg DXM Freebase, THCA Vape

# Comeup

> Dosed the dxm and been steadily vaping thca while I waited for it to kick in. Felt the typical body high of weed, body feels very warm and fuzzy, then after about an hour I started feeling the dxm. The body load shifted from being warm and fuzzy to numb and distant. Started to lose feeling in my body and forget I had arms and legs as I started to get vertigo. Once it started kicking in more and my mind was getting more distant from my body I turned off my pc and laid down in bed.

# Approaching Peak

> Started to feel nauseous so I put on headphones and started listening to music, music sounded highly detailed and a part of my body. Weed makes it just sound cool and three dimensional almost so this was on another level. Closing my eyes I felt like I was no longer listening to music but my mind became one with the music and thats all there was in the world. I never actually threw up at all, the nausea kept building but it eventually passed.

# Peak

> Other trips on dxm I've felt like my mind starts to float above outside of my body and I enter this large dark void in the shape of a room with a dome on it. This time it was almost the opposite, I felt like I was sinking down into my body / bed, and instead of entering a room the dark void you see when you close your eyes started growing until it overtook my entire field of view. I no longer was apart of my body but floating in a void of music and warmth. At this point I was listening to shpongles album "codex vi".

> The music was no longer music, it was like I was experiencing a movie in my mind. I saw super infinitely repeating colorful lines that resembled a kaleidoscope on a very dark background. They flowed and pulsed with the 3D direction of the music as I sank deeper and deeper into my body. Eventually the music had some water / ocean sounds to it and next thing I knew I was floating under the sea watching bubbles and water currents above me. As it kept progressing the visuals turned from imagery to what I thought was the "fabric of the universe". A dark void with these lines and shapes sometimes stretching to reveal colorful lines beyond the void. They were flowing and streaming with an infinitely repeating pattern. I remember the colors were very bright purples, blues, reds, and sometimes yellow.

# Comedown

> Comedown was nice, I shifted to listening to local h on spotify until I got tired and fell asleep. I woke up an hour or two later and was still tripping mildly, everything was very dizzy and when I looked at my hands they looked massive. I kinda got worried because I thought they were swollen, then I looked at the rest of my body and it looked like I was looking into a fun mirror, body parts were different sizes; arms were short, hands big, it was very weird. Never experienced that before, I drank a bunch of water then went back to bed for the rest of the night.
