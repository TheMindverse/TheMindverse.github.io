- **Date:** November 12th, 2024
- **Body Weight:** 110 Pounds
- **Dosage:** 300mg DXM Freebase, THCA Vape

# Comeup

> Starting play some games of DRG while I waited for the dxm to kick in, taking hits from vape from time to time. Eventually in the middle of a mission it hit really hard all the sudden. Was super dizzy and couldn't focus, had to quit the mission. Started listening to some music at this point trying to figure out what to play when it kicks in more. Eventually I landed on a zen meditation playlist I made prior.

# Peak

> Because I was playing DRG beforehand, the closed eye visuals all started off as just imagery of DRG randomly generated caves; and I was flying through all of them endlessly. Eventually that turned away from video games to like hand drawn animation and art. It was actually pretty violent with blood and people fighting and turned into monsters. But it wasn't scary because it looked like it was drawn with colored pencil on paper. Was like a full on movie scene. I don't remember much visuals after that, the weed made it super hard to get immersed because it just killed my thoughts from taking off. I felt very calm and relaxed the whole time because of the music that was playing.

# Comedown

> After I peaked I switched my zen playlist to just a lofi beats playlist, it was very calming and I felt like my body was melting. Felt like I was getting tired so I turned off the music to try to sleep. Didn't end up sleeping at all that night, was just waiting for me to pass out but it never happened. Got up few times to take more hits from vape to see if that would help and it didn't.
