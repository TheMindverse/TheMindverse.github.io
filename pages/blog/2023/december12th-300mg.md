- **Date:** December 12th 2nd, 2023
- **Body Weight:** 109 Pounds
- **Dosage:** 300mg DXM Freebase

# Comeup

> Made sure to take on an empty stomach, didn't eat for 3-4 hours before so I felt it start to work after 25-30 minutes almost. Had some fun playing guitar and messing with effects, but I felt more nauseous than other trips on the comeup. Possibly because I was playing guitar standing up and usually I'm laying in bed listening to music at this point. Still had fun, I played some levels of deep rock too after this while I waited for it to kick in more.

# Approaching Peak

> At this point I was already laying in bed listening to music and I started to get the robo-itch, then I had a theory that the itch is actually just hot flashes due to the sudden increase of serotonin floating around in the brain. Deep breaths and focusing on the music helped pass it rather quickly. Was starting to get lost in the music as I could no longer feel anything and I was getting double vision, when of course I had to throw up. Puked once and then laid back down.

# Peak

> Visuals started to kick in and they were much more colorful than any other trips previously. I had been playing astroneer almost all week so the visuals started to represent that. It's a low poly voxel like game with alien planets and terrain. I started to see the same voxel like system form into fractals, and the lines connecting them were bright purple and glowing, and kept changing. After this I felt I had to throw up again and crawled out of bed and puked my guts up, this was much worse than the first time, since I hadn't eaten anything it was pure stomach bile and burned like hell. Not fun, but I eventually made it back to bed.

> After that the visuals turned from just colorful lines to full on 3D scenery, I was floating across alien like planets and terrain but they were all the same voxel like system, where I could see polygons connecting them all together. They were fully colored and I remembered that I was tripping and thought I should try looking around to see if I can see anything else. Every time I tried it kept throwing me out of the trance and I had to "refocus" and get lost floating again. It's more like a static camera that keeps moving around that your body/head follows. Previous trips I felt like I entered a dome like area, but this time it was more like I traveled through a flat wall into another space entirely. I remember it being flat kind of like a door.

# Comedown

> Visuals stopped being fully 3D and turned into these colorful blobs, something I've only ever seen on acid during comedowns which really surprised me. On acid it's much more bright, like you're looking into an actual array of lights. But this wasn't really illuminated, although I could still see large blobs of colors morphing and floating around. I fell asleep about an hour later after being on my phone but only slept 2-3 hours total. The rest of the day I was still super energized and felt great.
