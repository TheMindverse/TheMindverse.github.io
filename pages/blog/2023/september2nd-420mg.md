- **Date:** September 2nd, 2023
- **Body Weight:** 105 Pounds
- **Dosage:** 420mg DXM HBr

# Comeup

> Comeup was very pleasant compared to other trips, I sat down in bed with headphones ready and was jamming out having a blast listening to music. Closed my eyes and as time went on I slowly felt like my body was moving all around with the music. Fast drops and feelings like I was falling or flying, which caused me to feel nauseous. After it got more intense that's when I stopped the music and paused to throw up. After that I felt fine, laid back down in bed.

# Peak

> Visuals start to happen but they were very different from before, they were super spazzy and flashy. First just flashing lights really fast then it turns into kaleidoscope like patterns. Unlike acid they were not very complex or colorful, and I could make out the weird shapes as they kept flickering and flashing to the music. I attached images I found on google that closest resembled the pattern types. After that I could feel that I was starting to break through, its a familiar feeling when you've done it enough times. You can feel your brain start to slip away and forget you have a body and enter this very weird black void like room. You can't really see it, but you can feel you are there and no longer in reality. The room turned into a large dome like shape which has happened many times before and the same kaleidoscope like patterns were flashing on the ceiling of it.

# Comedown

> I was staring to come down but I was still "on the other side" but I could recognize it still and wasn't fully immersed. I thought to myself I wonder if this is what dying feels like, your consciousness separating from your body and floating away. So I repeated to myself "I'm dying" because I thought it would be funny. Then I really started to worry that I could be dying or ODing and I wouldn't know it. So I quickly took off my headphones and opened discord to take my mind of it. I had to close one of my eyes to read my phone because everything was crazy dizzy and all mismatched. It was like looking around with my eyes crossed and spinning in a circle.

# Summery

> This was a positive trip, nothing really insane happened like meeting a god like entity or traveling through alien planets; just after having some bad trips last time this was a lot of fun and a much needed break from reality. Had a blast listening to music.
