- **Date:** July 28th, 2023
- **Body Weight:** 103 Pounds
- **Dosage:** 360mg DXM HBr

# Comeup

> Incredibly sleep deprived, been up since yesterday and I dosed at 3pm. I couldn't feel my body and I kept yawning, my mind did not feel tired but I knew my body was exhausted. Listened to some music for about 30 min and decided I'd try and sleep while tripping and hope I wake up fine.

# Peak

> At this point I was under the covers trying to sleep and I think I did fall asleep for a bit, it felt like I was dreaming but I'm not sure if I really was or I was still tripping. Was having dreams just about talking to people in my house, felt like a really weird fever dream because things and places kept changing rapidly but for the most part it wasn't that crazy. I woke up after what felt like almost an entire week passed, I have dreams and memories of full on days going buy in that fever dream to only see on my phone only an hour passed. I woke up still fully tripping, dizzy and nauseous, and the reason I got up was because I thought I wasn't breathing and something was wrong.

# Comedown

> It was super hot in my room, mid 80s, and I felt like I couldn't breath. I was taking very large slow deep breaths to try to center myself but it felt like no air was getting in and my heart rate was crazy fast. I started to feel like I was going to pass out and my vision was getting fuzzy, on top of that I was on the verge of throwing up. I crawled out of bed to my mini fridge and put a can of soda on my forehead and neck because I knew I was overheating. I laid on the floor with my eyes closed trying to slow my breathing and heart rate down because it was still going very fast. I started to see stars and flashes like I was going to pass out, but some time passed and that went away. My head was cooled down and I felt okay enough to sit up, and then instantly threw up into my barf bucket. After that I realized my clothes and hair were absolutely soaking wet in sweat. I was definitely on the verge of having a heat stroke and had no clue because I couldn't feel my body.

# Summery

> Not the worst trip based on that time dilation thing and the fever dream aspect, that was an interesting experience. Not bad or really fun, just interesting. This trip overall could have ended badly, if I passed out and threw up unconscious I could have died or suffered a heat stroke.
